📋 How to Customise This Portfolio

01
Open data.js in any text editor (Notepad, VS Code, Notepad++).
02
Replace every [placeholder] with your real information — name, school, story paragraphs, achievement descriptions, and reflections.
03
Copy your photos into the correct folder: images/achievements/, images/certificates/, images/projects/, etc.
04
In data.js, update imagePath and certificateImagePath for each achievement to match your file names (e.g. "images/achievements/njco.jpg").
05
Update your profile photo path in STUDENT.profilePhoto.
06
Save data.js, then refresh index.html in your browser. All changes appear automatically — no other files need editing.
07
To add more achievements or projects, copy an existing block in data.js, paste it, and fill in your new content.
